Got Questions? We've Got Answers

What happens if I exceed my conversation limit? +
Don't worry! We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional conversation packs. We never cut off your service unexpectedly.
Can I change plans at any time? +
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.
Is there a free trial available? +
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on our Professional plan with no credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period.
What kind of support do you provide? +
We provide comprehensive support including email support for all plans, priority support for Professional users, and dedicated support managers for Enterprise customers. Our knowledge base and video tutorials are available 24/7.
Do you offer custom pricing for high-volume users? +
Yes! If you need more than what our Enterprise plan offers, we'd love to create a custom solution for you. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
